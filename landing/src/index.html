oracle and blockchains codependency

Why blockchains need oracle

Blockchains are great at providing immutable storage and deterministic verifiable computations.

However, they cannot access trusted real-world information available outside their networks. Oracles enhances smart contract functionalities by granting access to reliable data without any central authority or points of failure.

Perspective

Our solution

A lack of a decentralized price aggregator motivates us to develop a platform that provides all necessary prices of cryptocurrencies to the Defi world. Moreover, with advanced technologies, our users are granted all security concerns and feel secure that the data they are using is trustworthy and valid.

Some facts

Token Utility

Validation

To maintain security considerations and to ensure data reliability, all validators must stake DOracle tokens.

Staking

Those who participate in stake pools can yield their funds and contribute to the data validation process.

Governance

The holders of DOracle tokens may vote on protocol upgrades and parameter adjustments they want to see in the network

Intermediary

DOracle tokens will be used for payment of transactions or query fees. In addition, API marketplace can also be used.
